To preface, this isn’t meant to serve as precedent for future cases nor should it be viewed as more than a one-off exception for context surrounding the situation. The rule as written is not being changed at this time.

Given a binary rule was not applied as such, I’ll detail my own thought process as the events surrounding the game transpired.

Very shortly before the game began, I was dmed by a third party about zS inquiring if they could have the timer turned off for the game due to ima’s internet allegedly not being stable. By the time I responded that the timer would not be turned off, the game had already started, and so the message wasn’t even passed to zS until after both players were in the match.

ima ultimately disconnected, but they were unable to reconnect before the their disconnect timer expired. Given they disconnected and were ineligible for a recreate, it should’ve been a cookie-cutter timer win per the rules exactly as written, i.e. a forced recreate was not being considered.

However, I had been made more aware of the conversation between zS and ima as the game itself and subsequent events transpired, including the aforementioned connectivity issues, as well as zS immediately and repeatedly signaling they’d prefer a recreate and multiple members of his team stating they were behind him, it being semifinals of a team trophy tour, and the disconnect-in my own opinion-not seeming malicious, I opted to pivot and bend the rule, instead offering Italy as a whole the timer win per the rules as written or to play it out. As accepting the recreate could and/or would disadvantage Italy as a whole, this is why I did not solely make the offer to zS but first to him and subsequently tagged the rest of team Italy on Discord, and regardless of the overwhelming likelihood it would (and did) end in Italy claiming the win, it was offered as what I, at the time, believed to be the fairest way possible to maybe see the game played at all without upending the rule completely. You can call it my mistake, as I’m the one who ultimately both suggested and offered it.

Any assumption the figurative buck was passed is just that - an assumption; the only intent here was to give whatever small percentage of a chance there was to have the game played out, and any potential judgment anyone has reserved or rendered towards Italy should be directed towards me alone. As mentioned above, this is a one-off given the ample context surrounding the situation and should not be used in decision making in the future.

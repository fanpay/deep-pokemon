After four weeks of big and intense competition and frustration spent on the ladder, here we are to the playoffs of the eleventh Official Ladder Tournament. There's been a lot of things going on over the last four cycles, so let's give everyone a show in playoffs too! The competition will be fierce and we all hope we'll get to see interesting and high-level SV OU battles between you all.

This playoff bracket will start as a swiss tournament. As such, you continue playing in this stage until you have won or lost three matches in the playoffs stage.

Remember, all matches are to be played in a BEST OF THREE format, and all standard SV OU rules apply.

Finally, before going into the pairings, we'd like to thank the PS Staff, from the owners to the moderators, for making the ladder phase of the tournament possible and smoother in its running. We appreciate your help and support, and a special thanks to pre and Mia for making this a lot better. Now, without further ado...
